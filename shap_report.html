Methodology
XGBoost — TreeExplainer (exact) SHAP TreeExplainer runs exact game-theoretic attribution across all 8,496 test samples — no sampling approximation. Global importance = mean |SHAP| aggregated across all samples and all tickers.
LSTM / TFT — GradientExplainer (approximate) GradientExplainer requires a differentiable forward pass. Both models are wrapped as thin nn.Module stubs so SHAP can back-propagate gradients through the sequence. 200 background samples provide the reference distribution; 1,000 test samples are explained. Each sample has shape [30 timesteps × 20 features] — SHAP values are averaged over the 30-step sequence dimension to yield one score per feature, matching the XGBoost format.
