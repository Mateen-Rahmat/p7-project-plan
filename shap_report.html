Key Findings

⚡

Volatility dominates universally

volatility_10 is the #1 feature across all three models (avg importance 0.112). Recent price turbulence is the strongest short-term directional signal regardless of architecture.

🌐

XGBoost ignores macro entirely

XGBoost assigns 0.000 SHAP to yield_curve and DXY — tree splits saturate on per-ticker price features. LSTM and TFT exploit macro spreads strongly via sequence memory.

⏱

Each model learns a different lag

XGBoost leans on ret_1 (immediate momentum); TFT favours ret_3; LSTM favours ret_5. Architecture shapes which time-scale of return is most predictive.

💬

Sentiment ranks 18th of 20

fused_sentiment avg importance = 0.030 — real and non-zero, but well below price-derived signals. Its marginal contribution is meaningful; it is not the dominant driver.

📐

RSI: universal mean-reversion signal

RSI ranks #2 overall (avg 0.084) and scores highly in both XGBoost and TFT (~0.11). Mean-reversion awareness is learned independently by all three architectures.

✅

MACD: the only full-consensus feature

MACD is the single indicator that ranks in the top 5 for all three models individually — confirming its role as the most robust trend signal across architecture families.

Methodology

how SHAP values were computed per model family
XGBoost — TreeExplainer (exact) SHAP TreeExplainer runs exact game-theoretic attribution for all 8,496 test samples — no sampling approximation. Global importance = mean |SHAP| aggregated across all samples and all tickers.
LSTM / TFT — GradientExplainer (approximate) GradientExplainer requires a differentiable forward pass. Both models are wrapped as thin nn.Module stubs so SHAP can back-propagate gradients through the sequence. 200 background samples provide the reference distribution; 1,000 test samples are explained. Each explained sample has shape [30 timesteps × 20 features] — SHAP values are averaged over the 30-step sequence dimension to yield one importance score per feature, matching the XGBoost format for cross-model comparison.
